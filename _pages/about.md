I am currently pursuing an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University. I conduct research in the Biomimetics and Dexterous Manipulation Lab ([BDML](https://bdml.stanford.edu/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/HomePage)) under the guidance of [Prof. Mark Cutkosky](https://bdml.stanford.edu/pmwiki/pmwiki.php/Main/HomePage), where my work focuses on developing whisker-inspired tactile sensors for underwater robotics. I received my dual B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Ajou University and the Illinois Institute of Technology. During my undergraduate studies, I was advised by [Prof. Yan](https://tomyan555.github.io) on deep multimodal learning, [Prof. Bhushan](https://sites.google.com/iit.edu/bhushan-research-group/people) on control systems with Reinforcement learning, and [Prof. Koh](http://most.ajou.ac.kr) on Dielectric Elastomer actuator(DEA) research.

I previously received my dual B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Ajou University and Illinois Institute of Technology, where I developed a strong foundation in both mechanical engineering and artificial intelligence.
